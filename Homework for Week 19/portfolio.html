    RidMe: What can I do with my stuff?

    Project Overview

    Our team was asked to design an application that would assist local residents with daily issues. We decided to focus on recycling because everyone knows at least one person that sometimes does not understand what to do with certain items.

    Team Size+ Role & Responsibility

    UX Designers: Srujana Munamala, Samantha Woehrle, Lara Garrison

    Tools used: Adobe XD, Miro

    Timeline & Scope

    Our team spent about 3 weeks on this project. The timeline seemed short, but we managed to complete the tasks.

    Problem Statement

    Many eco-conscious consumers have the desire to donate or recycle as much as possible but become befuddled by complicated rules regarding compost/recycling and lack of knowledge of local donation options for items that thrift stores will not accept. This issue increases the amount of otherwise-useful garbage that ends up in landfills and engenders guilt and frustration in the consumer. A successful solution would provide easily-attainable, fast and straightforward guidance about ideal disposal of household items.
